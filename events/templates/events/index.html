Why play at the Magic Corner?

The Magic Corner gives players the opportunity to play new games with other players in their community. It's a great way to meet new people! Don't worry, if you've never played before we will teach you. Snacks and drinks are available for purchase during each game. We also have cards, premade decks, game books, and game accessories available for purchase in our store!


How to join or add a game:

In our websites navigation menu, you will see a dropdown for games. Click on the dropdown and from there you can see more information on scheduled games, schedule a game, or request to join a game. Just fill out the provided forms and we will let you know if there are any issues!
